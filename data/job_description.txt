Job Title: Senior AI Engineer — Search & Retrieval Systems
Company: Nexus AI Labs
Location: Remote (India preferred)
Type: Full-time

COMPANY OVERVIEW
Nexus AI Labs builds next-generation AI search and knowledge retrieval products for enterprise clients. Our platform processes billions of vectors daily and helps organizations unlock the value of their unstructured data using state-of-the-art LLMs and vector databases.

ROLE OVERVIEW
We are looking for a Senior AI Engineer with deep expertise in large-scale retrieval systems, LLM integration, and ML infrastructure to join our core platform team. You will design, build, and optimize the RAG pipelines that power our flagship product.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design and implement production-grade Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines from scratch.
- Own the vector database infrastructure: indexing, querying, optimization, and scaling.
- Integrate and evaluate open-source and commercial LLMs (Mistral, LLaMA, GPT-4) for domain-specific use cases.
- Build robust document ingestion pipelines: chunking strategies, embedding generation, metadata filtering.
- Collaborate with product and infrastructure teams to serve low-latency AI search APIs at scale.
- Conduct benchmark experiments and drive model selection decisions using data.
- Mentor junior engineers and establish AI engineering best practices across the organization.

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
- 4+ years of experience in Python development and machine learning engineering.
- Strong hands-on experience building and deploying RAG systems in production.
- Proficiency with vector databases: Endee, Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, or FAISS.
- Experience with embedding models: sentence-transformers, OpenAI embeddings, or equivalent.
- Familiarity with LLM frameworks: LangChain, LlamaIndex, or direct API usage.
- Solid understanding of HNSW, ANN search, and approximate nearest neighbor algorithms.
- Experience with containerized deployments (Docker, Kubernetes).

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
- Contributions to open-source AI or ML projects.
- Experience with Ollama or other local LLM inference solutions.
- Familiarity with MLOps pipelines, model observability, and experiment tracking.
- Prior experience at a high-growth startup or AI product company.

COMPENSATION
- Competitive salary: ₹30L – ₹55L per annum (based on experience)
- Equity participation
- Remote-first culture with flexible working hours

HOW TO APPLY
Submit your resume and a brief note explaining your most impactful RAG or vector search project to careers@nexus-ai.io.
